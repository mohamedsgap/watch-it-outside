Watch it OutSide

Boston edition

About this event:

Why choose between watching the starry night sky and a good film? Boston’s public garden, squares and open spaces provide the perfect opportunity to enjoy movie nights in the great urban outdoors. As soon as the weather allows, film screens pop up all over the city.From one-time events and film festivals to full programmes throughout August, we’ve selected some of our favorite outdoor cinemas in United States. Grab your friends, a cold beer, a picnic blanket and enjoy the show!

Shows will be free and open to everyone, but you need to pre-register so that we are able to estimate how many people might come and so that we can close registration if it reaches the venue’s capacity of 1,500 people.
